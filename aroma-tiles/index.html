About Us

New to the neighborhood,
built on an old craft.

Aroma Tiles opened its doors as a fresh branch dedicated to one thing: helping people choose the right tile for the right space, without the guesswork.

Though we're a new branch — less than a year in — our approach is anything but untested. We stock a tight, well-considered range of floor, wall, bathroom and ceramic tiles, and we spend real time with every customer to understand the room, the light, the foot traffic, and the budget before recommending a single tile.
